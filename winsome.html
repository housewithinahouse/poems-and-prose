Winsome Slow black trails a windowsill, a multifidus marching line slow slipping home in moist poison, to roost in my chest, down multi-branching mega highways alongside my spine I sit and stumble, a confused shambling shuffle to rest, as it courses those forks without a single tine. Various visions threaten at the corners of existence, killing coordination through some series of calculated steps designed for the provocation of flight. Frightful resistance to our plans, I know I can.
