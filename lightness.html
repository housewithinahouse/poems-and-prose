A lightness in the air, a spring in my step. Sun shining, night falls On antipodal points at orthogonal times. Here I am, with heavens spinning overhead, Ground tilting beneath feet. I am a dervish, And the world pirouettes around me. Slow, steady progression of titanic plates Across, under, and besides the one I call home Causes fractures and splits in it's surface. The very ground I stand on has been subsumed, And is consuming others. Nothing remains untouched by this procession.
